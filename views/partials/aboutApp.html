To know about our history

From home page, please click on "Hear our Story".
From any other page, please click the bottom button 'About-i'.

To view the list of menus available

From home page, please click on any of the categories you like from 'Today's Specials' and you will be able to view the list of menu's for that category.
For example, you can click on "Appetizers" and you will be able to view the list of menu's under appetizer.
From any other page, please click on top most left "Menu" button and it will list the categories from which you might want to select the menus

To add Orders

Click on the specific category you like. Once the menu's are listed, click on 'add' and it will be added to the order.
To add one more quantity, click on "add to order' again.

To remove Orders

Click on the specific category where you want to remove the order. Once the menu's are listed, look for the menu that you want to remove and click on 'remove' if its already added to your order

To reviews Orders

Please, click on top most left 'Menu' button and click on 'Review the order'. New page will be displayed with all your orders added. You can review them

To cancel Orders

Please, click on "Your Order' button and click on 'Cancel' button.

To place Orders

Please, click on "Your Order' button and click on 'send order' button. You can also click on the 'Menu' button and click on 'Review Your Order' and then press 'send order' button.

To check your orders placed

Please, click on "Your Order' button.

To get Assistance

From Home page, click on '?' button at the top right corner.
From any other page, please, click on 'Help' button at the bottom of the page.

To talk to waiter

From Home page, click on '?' button at the top right corner. Click on 'Call your Host' at the bottom page.
From any other page, please, click on 'Call your Host' at the bottom page.
